What is Good Citizenship

Dicitonary.com defines citizenship as the characer of an individual viewed as a member pf society; behavior and terms of the duties,obligations and function of a citizen. I believe that honesty, compassion, respect, responsibility, and courage are necessary components of good citizenship.

Characteristics of Good Citizenship

Honesty is truthfulness, sincerity, or frankness[dictionary.com]. Honesty should be a component of good citizenship because it allows people in a community to trust each other. Compassion is a feeling of deep sympathy and sorrow for another who is strickenby misfortune, accompanied by a strong desire to allieviate suffering[dictionary.com]. This is important because it allows a group to feel more connecting knowing that other people care about you. Respect is esteem or a sense of the worth of or excellence of a person, a person quaity or ability, or something considered as a manifestation of a personal quality or ability[dictionary.com]. This is essential in order to have good citizenship because if respect can give a community kinder or more forgiving if you respect them and they respect you. Responsibility is the state or fact of being responsible, answerable, or accountable for something within one's power, control, or management[dictionary.com]. This is important for good citizenship because it helps keep people from doing wrong or being lazy. Courage is the quality of mind or spirit that enables a person to fave difficulty, danger, pain, etc./ with fear; bravery[Dictionary.com]. Courage helps keep the community running because it makes people go out of their comfort zone to make the group better. All these characteristics are assential for good citizenship because they keep the population safe and running efficently.

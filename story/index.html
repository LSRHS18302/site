Background

You have finished high school and are deciding whether or not you want to go to college. You have a good GPA of 3.5 and a SAT score of 1300. Your parents really want you to go to college and will pay for you whole college tuition. Going to college would mean taking at least another 4 years of school, but you would be able to get into a better paying job. Skipping college and getting a job would mean you would be done with school and would start making money.

Do you want to go to college or get a job?
